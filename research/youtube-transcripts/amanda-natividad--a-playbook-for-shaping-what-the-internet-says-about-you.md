## Key ideas captured

- In modern discovery, people form opinions from the public record before they ever click a website.
- Brands need stronger proof, not just stronger claims.
- Content must be easy to retrieve, cite, and summarize.
- Reputation shaping now includes Reddit threads, reviews, snippets, and AI answers.
