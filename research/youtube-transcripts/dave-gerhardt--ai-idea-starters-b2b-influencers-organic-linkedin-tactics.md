## Key ideas captured

- Social media should be treated as a primary B2B SaaS marketing channel, not a side tactic.
- Founder brand can outperform traditional launch PR because it provides direct access to the relevant audience.
- AI is best used as an idea starter and workflow accelerator, not as a replacement for strategic thinking.
- B2B influencer programs work best when they match niche audience trust, not just reach.
