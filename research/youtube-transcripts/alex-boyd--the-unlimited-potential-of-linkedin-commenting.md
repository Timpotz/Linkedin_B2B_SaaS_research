## Key ideas captured

- Commenting can outperform posting as a growth lever on LinkedIn.
- Rebranding yourself in public is often about repetition, not one big announcement.
- LinkedIn content systems should reduce workflow friction, not maximize polish.
- Half-baked ideas and live learning often outperform over-processed thought leadership.
- Metrics that matter include profile views, DMs, and business outcomes, not just likes.
